# Frameworks

Seven operating frameworks that sit **above** any project-specific skill. They govern design,
collaboration, research, craft, QA, and integration decisions across the entire workspace.

Orientation: [[00-frameworks/00-README|00-README]] — compressed summaries and when each framework applies.

## The seven

1. **[[00-frameworks/01-aesthetic-lens|Aesthetic Lens]]** — philosophical ground, visual/aesthetic judgment
2. **[[00-frameworks/02-ui-ux-operational-framework|UI/UX Operational Framework]]** — operational decisions for interface + experience
3. **[[00-frameworks/03-collaboration-and-critique-framework|Collaboration and Critique Framework]]** — how to collaborate, give critique, handle handoff
4. **[[00-frameworks/04-research-and-evidence-framework|Research and Evidence Framework]]** — epistemology, evidence standards, how conclusions are warranted
5. **[[00-frameworks/05-last-mile-craft-framework|Last-Mile Craft Framework]]** — finishing discipline, augmented perception
6. **[[00-frameworks/06-qa-operating-model|QA Operating Model]]** — target-user expectations as the QA bar on first delivery
7. **[[00-frameworks/07-integration-and-review-framework|Integration & Review Framework]]** — branching, PRs, and consolidation partitioned for easy review
